Biotec Italia awarded at World of Beauty in Prague
Thursday, 10 September 2015

Mesotherm got the BEPPA at the 20th World of Beauty in Prague

Biotec Italia is proud to annouce that Mesotherm got the BEPPA at the 20th World of Beauty, thanks to the precious collaboration of its local distributor Medi Health and Beauty


The BEPPA (Beauty Expo & Prokos Products Award) awards high quality products, and Mesotherm got the prize for the following reasons:

"Very comfortable and modern using modern technological processes. The device operates on the principle of combined effects of microdermabrasion technology, diathermy, electroporation and vacuum. Treatment using MESOTHERM is time-consuming and provides clients visible results after just a few treatments. It allows to treat skin with clients of various ages. Convenient, hygienic, can be combined with other aesthetic surgery. With additional cosmeceutical products, allow treated person to continue a following home care.
Assessment: MESOTHERM The device is suitable for a wide range of treatments, meet hygiene and safety standards.
Exhibitor: MEDI Health and Beauty Ltd."

World of Beauty is the international trade fair for comestics, hairdressing and healthy lifestyle which is be held twice every year: Spring (March) and Autumn (September). Competitions are the important part of the fair. The most traditional of these are the Czech championship in make-up, Czech and International Championships in Nail Design, Pedicure, big hairdressers competition INSPIRATION 2016 and also the awarding of best cosmetic products BEAUTY EXPO AND PROKOS PRODUCTS AWARD. the one obtained by Mesotherm

Mesotherm is a multifunctional platform providing effective treatment of wrinkles, hyperkeratosis, acne and enlarged pores, thickened skin, hyperpigmentation marks, stretch marks, scars and baldness.
Discover what Mesotherm can do to improve people skin and the whole medical devices catalogue.

Biotec Italia did not bring home only this award: indeed, the jury at the World of Beauty awarded also a Renlive cosmeceutical product.
The Reverse Age Defying Cream got the BEPPA with the following reason:

"Cream containing proline, retinol, arbutin, cocoa butter, almond oil, and squalene provides skin nutrition and hydration. The product is well absorbed after application to the skin without leaving a greasy feel and smell nice."

The Reverse Age Defying Cream is a biotechnological moisturizer with active ingredients that nourish, protect and moisturize the skin. Smooth out wrinkles and restore the elasticity and firmness of the skin with glycoprotein and proline, as they prevent the ageing process of the skin.

The World of Beauty Autumn 2015 has been a really succes. By one side, the event demonstrated the excellent results of the collaboration between Biotec Italia and its Czech distributor, Medi Health and Beauty. By another side awarded the Biotec Italia's constant efforts to find the best solution to enhance and protect people skin.
